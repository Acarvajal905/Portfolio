CARCHAT

Carchat is a project that allows drivers to receive real-time notifications and warnings about their cars based on updates from other drivers. By using Carchat, drivers can gain access to important information about their vehicles, such as unpaid bills, insurance status, and maintenance needs. Also, you can have information provided by other drivers who may have noticed something about the car that the owner did not. Carchat helps ensure that cars are always in good condition and ready to be driven when needed.
